ABOUT US

Based in Nashik, Maharashtra, we offer expertise in electioneering, policy formulation, and governance advisory. With over five years of experience, we have assisted more than 300 clients and supported over 500 assemblies, establishing ourselves as trusted advisors in the political landscape.

Our team blends seasoned political consultants and diverse professionals, each bringing unique expertise. We specialize in policy recommendations, grassroots campaigns, stakeholder alignment, impactful events, social media engagement, and strategic multimedia content. We aim to foster dialogue and collaboration among young professionals to revolutionize electioneering, policy-making, and governance advisory.

Rooted in strategic planning and organization at all levels, we emphasize research and technology to inform our campaign plans. Founded by a visionary leader with experience in CAG and IPAC, Impact Axis Consulting blends modern and traditional techniques to stay people-centric. Our Founder

Pravin Gangurde

Founder & CEO

Pravin Gangurde is the Founder and Director of ImpactAxis Consulting, a firm specializing in Political Consulting, Governance Strategy, Digital PR, Policy Research, Business Consulting, and Brand Promotion. An alumnus of IIT Bombay, where he earned his Master’s in Public Policy, and Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pravin brings a rare blend of academic insight and field expertise. Over the past five years, he has worked with leading political consultancies such as IPAC, STC, Mind Share, and Hudds, driving impactful campaigns and strategies across Maharashtra, Karnataka, Bihar, Bengal, and Sikkim. Known for his visionary approach and grounded understanding of socio-political dynamics, Pravin has collaborated with major political leaders to design innovative, result-oriented solutions that bridge policy, people, and performance.
